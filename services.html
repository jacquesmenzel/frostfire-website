‚ùÑÔ∏è Ductless Mini-Split Installation

Ductless mini-split systems offer targeted heating and cooling without ductwork. They're ideal for home additions, converted garages, sunrooms, older homes, and commercial spaces.

Where Mini-Splits Shine

  • Home additions and renovations
  • Converted garages, attics, or basements
  • Older homes without existing ductwork
  • Sunrooms and bonus rooms
  • Home offices & commercial spaces
  • Restaurants and server rooms

Benefits

  • No ductwork required ‚Äî easier, less invasive installation
  • Zone control ‚Äî heat or cool only the rooms you're using
  • Energy efficient ‚Äî no duct losses (which waste 20-30% of energy)
  • Quiet operation with individual temperature control per room

We install single-zone and multi-zone systems (up to 5 indoor units connected to one outdoor unit).
