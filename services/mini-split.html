Ductless Comfort Where You Need It Most

Mini-split systems have revolutionized home comfort by providing efficient, flexible heating and cooling without the need for ductwork. At Frost Fire Heating and Cooling, we specialize in ductless mini-split installation and repair throughout Clayton, Raleigh, Durham, and the greater Triangle area. Whether you need to heat and cool a garage workshop, a new home addition, a sunroom, or an older home without existing ductwork, mini-splits offer an elegant, energy-efficient solution.

A ductless mini-split system consists of an outdoor compressor unit connected to one or more indoor air handlers via a small conduit. This means installation is minimally invasive ‚Äî no tearing open walls or ceilings to run ductwork. Most single-zone installations can be completed in just a few hours, with only a small 3-inch hole needed through an exterior wall. Multi-zone systems allow you to connect multiple indoor units to a single outdoor unit, providing independent temperature control in different rooms.

Mini-splits are incredibly energy efficient, often achieving SEER2 ratings above 20 ‚Äî far higher than most traditional central air systems. Because they deliver conditioned air directly into each room without the energy losses associated with ductwork, they can reduce heating and cooling costs by 25-40% compared to conventional systems. They're also whisper-quiet, with indoor units operating at just 19-25 decibels ‚Äî quieter than a library.

Transparent Mini-Split Pricing

Mini-split costs depend on the number of zones, system capacity (BTU), brand, and any electrical work required. Single-zone installations are the most affordable option, while multi-zone systems cost more but provide whole-home coverage. We provide detailed written estimates so you know exactly what you're paying for ‚Äî equipment, labor, materials, and permits.
