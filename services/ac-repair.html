Common AC Problems We Fix Every Day

AC Blowing Warm Air

Low refrigerant, compressor issues, or thermostat problems can cause your AC to blow warm air. We'll find and fix the root cause fast.

Strange Noises

Grinding, squealing, or banging sounds often indicate loose parts, motor problems, or compressor failure. Don't ignore these warning signs.

Water Leaks

Clogged condensate drains, frozen evaporator coils, or damaged drain pans can cause water to leak from your AC unit, potentially causing water damage.

System Won't Turn On

Electrical issues, tripped breakers, faulty thermostats, or blown capacitors can prevent your AC from starting. We troubleshoot all electrical components.

Short Cycling

When your AC turns on and off frequently, it wastes energy and wears out components. Causes include overheating, refrigerant leaks, or oversized units.

High Energy Bills

A sudden spike in your electric bill often signals an inefficient AC system. Dirty filters, duct leaks, or failing components may be to blame.
