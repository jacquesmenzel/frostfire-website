Signs Your System Needs a Tune-Up

Rising Energy Bills

If your utility bills are climbing without a change in usage, your system is likely losing efficiency due to dirty coils, clogged filters, or worn components.

Weak Airflow

Reduced airflow from your vents often points to dirty blower components, clogged filters, or ductwork issues that a tune-up can identify and resolve.

Unusual Sounds

Rattling, humming, or squealing noises indicate loose parts, failing bearings, or worn belts ‚Äî all caught during a routine maintenance visit.

It's Been Over a Year

Manufacturers recommend annual professional maintenance to maintain warranty coverage and ensure safe, efficient operation of your HVAC system.

Dust or Odors

Excess dust, musty smells, or stale air circulating through your home signals dirty coils, mold growth, or contaminated ductwork.

Short Cycling

If your system turns on and off frequently, maintenance can address dirty sensors, refrigerant issues, or airflow restrictions causing the problem.
