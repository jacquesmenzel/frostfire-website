Common Furnace Problems We Solve

Furnace Won't Ignite

Pilot light issues, faulty igniters, gas valve problems, or thermocouple failures. We diagnose and repair all ignition system components.

Blowing Cold Air

Thermostat issues, dirty flame sensors, clogged filters, or ductwork problems can cause your furnace to blow cool or lukewarm air.

Strange Noises

Banging, rattling, screeching, or booming sounds from your furnace indicate mechanical issues that need prompt professional attention.

Short Cycling

When your furnace turns on and off rapidly, it could be overheating due to a dirty filter, faulty thermostat, or cracked heat exchanger.

Yellow Pilot Light

A yellow or flickering pilot light (instead of blue) can indicate a carbon monoxide issue. This requires immediate professional inspection.

Poor Air Quality

Dusty, dry, or stale air from your furnace can signal dirty filters, cracked heat exchangers, or ductwork contamination.
